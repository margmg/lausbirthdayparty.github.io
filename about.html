Birthdays

Some people throw birthday parties once a year, I throw them all the time.

Lau’s Birthday Party is a curated gig series that celebrates the fresh talent of London’s guitar scene, prioritising the artists vision; putting the music first. A selection of events pieced together from an avid fan of London's music scene, this is a showcase from a music lover from the inside.

The party isn’t thrown just once a year. What started as a birthday celebration has turned into a showcase of London's eclectic up and coming. Why celebrate just once?

Here we are, welcome to Lau’s Birthday Party.

Run by me, Lau — a promoter, booker, and live music fanatic. I curate lineups that bring together the new, from underground gems to future festival headliners. If you’re here to discover your new favorite band or just looking for a great night of live music full of like-minded people, you’ve come to the right party.
